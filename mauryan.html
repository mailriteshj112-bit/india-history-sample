Founded by Chandragupta Maurya, this was the first empire to unify most of the Indian subcontinent. It reached its zenith under Emperor Ashoka.

Why it matters

Under Ashoka, Buddhism spread across Asia. The Lion Capital of Ashoka, built during this time, is now the national emblem of modern India.

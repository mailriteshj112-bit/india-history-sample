The Indus Valley Civilisation was a Bronze Age civilisation in the northwestern regions of South Asia. It was one of three early civilisations of the Near East and South Asia.

Why it matters

They developed the world's first known urban sanitation systems. The ancient Indus systems of sewerage and drainage were far more advanced than any found in contemporary urban sites in the Middle East.
